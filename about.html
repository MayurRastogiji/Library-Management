About Library Hub

Your comprehensive digital library for academic resources, handwritten notes, and study materials designed to accelerate your learning journey.

Our Story

Library Hub was born from a simple yet powerful idea shared by Lucky Tomar and brought to life by Mayur Rastogi. We recognized the constant struggle students face in accessing quality study materials and the recurring question: "Can you please send me the notes?"

This platform bridges that gap by providing instant access to a comprehensive collection of handwritten notes, question papers, assignments, and study materials - all curated and organized for easy navigation.
